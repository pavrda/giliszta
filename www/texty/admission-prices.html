  • Groups of 10 and more people receive a 10 percent discount. In the case of school groups, supervising teachers have free admission (provided there is no more than 1 adult supervisor for every 10 children or, in the case of preschoolers, no more than 1 adult supervisor for every 5 children).

  • Holders of Family Passports (“Rodinné pasy”) receive a 10 percent discount on the one-day ticket price for adults and children, provided they show a valid card.
